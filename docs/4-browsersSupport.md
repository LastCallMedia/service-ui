# Browsers Support

- Google Chrome - 62 and higher.
- Mozila Firefox - 61 and higher.
- Safari - 11 and higher.
- Edge - 16 and higher.
- Opera - 54 and higher.

Internet Explorer - not supported as its being deprecated by Microsoft.
